S=Vit+1/2at2: The Equation of Linear Motion

S=Vit+1/2at2 describe the motion of an object under constant acceleration

  • S represent the displacement
  • Vi is the initial velocity
  • t is the time
  • a is the accleration,and
  • 1/2at2 is the additional displacement due to accleration

    • An object in motion stays in motion unless acted upon by an external force.
